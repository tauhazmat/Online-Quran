How the courses are designed?

Our courses are designed according to the age and abilities of learners. We designed the course outlined especially for kids and for beginners in which they start from Noorani Qaida. In our online Quran classes for kids, we especially emphasize Tajweed. From the first day, the teacher helps students to pronounce the word with the correct pronunciation so that when they move forward, they recite in Tajweed intuitively. We know online teaching is different from teaching in person, so all our Quran courses are 1 to 1 to make sure student is paying attention to their lessons.

Why choose us?

We take all the responsibility for you and your kids. We take you to step by step with your children at the highest level to learn and to read the Quran online with Tajweed at the comfort of your home and your selected timings, supervision of parents, and guidance of expert Quran Teachers makes online Quran learning a lot easier. Therefore, you can rely on our Online Quran Classes for Kids.

FAQ's

Can I choose class timing at my own convenience?

Yes, you can. Our tutors are available 24/7 so you can pick any time as per your convenience.

Do you have female tutors as well?

Yes, we have Qariahs too those female teachers are equally qualified, and they are Hafiza too.

What is the duration of each class?

Each class lasts about 30-35 minutes. The duration may be longer for some of the lessons depending on the topics covered.

Can I change class timing once I pick up the time?

No, you can’t. Once you pick up the time as per your convenience you can’t change it.

What if I need to change my class timing for some reason?

In this case, you need to send us the email asking to change the timing.

Do you have family discounts?

Yes, there is discount for two or more than two members of the family.

How do you collect fee?

We accept payment through any debit/credit card.

Can I cancel my subscription?

You may cancel your subscription any time, but it would be helpful to the tutor if you provided 1-month notice.

Do I need a physical copy of The Quran?

No. We will provide all the course materials such as Digital Quran, Noorani Qaida etc. A tutor can highlight the passage/line he/she is reciting on his screen using a mouse and it will get highlighted on your screen so you can follow where your tutor is pointing.
